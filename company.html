Parucious is a premium beef lung chip that combines authentic flavors with traditional recipes and modern innovation. Available in Original, BBQ, and Spicy variants, Parucious offers a unique snacking experience with the finest ingredients and hygienic production processes. Each bite prioritizes authentic taste, making it a perfect snack choice for everyone. Now available on online platforms, Parucious makes it easy for customers to enjoy these delicious Indonesian-style beef lung chips, bringing the rich flavors of the archipelago right to their fingertips.
